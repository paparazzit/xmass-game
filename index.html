Ho Ho Ho! It’s the most wonderful time of the year!
Christmas around the world is celebrated with many unique and peculiar traditions.
One such tradition comes from Catalonia, in which a cute little log poops out gifts for children on Christmas Eve!
Play a little game to meet Tió de Nadal, the Catalan Christmas Log!
He might have a special gift just for you, if you can figure out what you need to do!
